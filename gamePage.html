The Lost Pirate

A 2D Game of Island Exploration and Mystery

Chap.0 - From the Sea

You and your crew get stuck in a storm of weird proportions. As the storm grows heavier, you lose consciousness. When you wake up, you find yourself in the depths of the ship, armed only with your trusty sword and gun.

Chap.1 - The Grand “Tuorial”

As you stand up and call around for anyone to hear… strangely there is no response. Assured that something really abnormal happened here in the ship you go towards the outside to find the ship stuck in the bay of a unknown island.
